ABOUT US

OUR MISSION

Firm Health Ghana Foundation is committed to decreasing the incidence of kidney disease and improving the lives of Ghanaians affected by kidney disease through:

1

OUR VISION

A society where renal disease no longer interferes with quality of life

2

ABOUT US

  • Increasing public awareness of the risk factors of kidney disease and empowering those diagnosed with kidney disease to optimize their health status;
  • Providing free renal screening at the community level; and
  • Advocating for improved access to high-quality health care for kidney patients

Who we are

Firm Health Ghana Foundation (FHGF), is a voluntary, non-governmental, non-partisan, non-profit, patient-focused, and humanitarian organization interested in the prevention of kidney disease by early detection through screening and awareness creation through education. It is duly registered with the Registrar General Department of Ghana and the department of social welfare, under the company’s act 1963 (act 179) as a company limited by guarantee in July 2012.

OUR CORE VALUES

We are guided by the values of Collaboration, Empathy, Volunteerism, and Team Excellence as a foundation and as individuals.

Our Impact

We are committed to being the lifeline to the end line of persons diagnosed with kidney disease. To this end, we have since 2013, initiated a weekly public-health education program on a local FM radio station known as Dynamite FM (88.9). The radio program reaches over 70,000 people in about 30 communities every week. The foundation strengthens the radio program by visiting communities to undertake free screening sessions. We have visited 29 communities and screened over 12,000 people. The Foundation reinforces its advocacy efforts through community forums and durbars to educate traditional authorities and local healthcare givers. We also organize bi-weekly diabetes and hypertension clinics to enlighten afflicted persons on management strategies of renal disease.
