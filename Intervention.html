OUR INTERVENTIONS

OUR INTERVENTIONS

Weekly Radio Program / Community Engagement & Screening / Monthly Health Walk

Weekly Radio Program

Firm Health Ghana Foundation initiated the weekly public health radio program in the year 2013 to educate the general public on the growing incidence of renal disease as well as other public health related issues in the Tarkwa-Nsuaem Municipality. The radio program takes place every Wednesday morning between the hours of 8:30 am and 9:30 am on Dynamite fm (88.9). The radio program is sphere-headed by Dr. Joseph Darko and Nurse In charge Ms. Gladys Fummey. The radio program reaches over 50,000 people in over 25 communities in the Tarkwa Nsuaem Municipality.

Community Engagement & Screening

The Community Engagement and Screening intervention was initiated in 2015 to strengthen the advocacy arm of the foundation. The intervention includes but not limited to engaging and educating civil society organizations, churches, junior and senior high schools, market women as well as companies on kidney health and its associated risk factors. Members of these organizations are also screened for early detection of kidney disease. The foundation has since the inception od this intervention has visited 23 communities and organizations and screened over 11,000 people.

Monthly Health Walk

The Monthly Health Walk intervention was initiated in 2016 with the sole aim for community members to live and maintain healthy lifestyles. The intervention is a 5KM health walk through some principal streets in the Tarkwa-Nsuaem Municipality. The entire health activity is climaxed with an aerobics session. The walk attracts over 400 people every month. Participants are then educated on some healthy and lifestyle tips from the medical doctors who are part of the foundation.
